# Journal

Occasional writing from the studio. Process notes, reference dumps, the occasional rant about lens choices or museum lighting. Subscribe via the contact form if you'd like new entries by email.

## What's here

Three rough categories of writing:

- **Practical notes** — how we approach a brief, what cameras we actually use, why we don't shoot RAW for everything
- **References** — books, exhibitions, buildings, films that keep coming up in studio conversations
- **Reflections** — slower thinking about the work itself, less about technique
