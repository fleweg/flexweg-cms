# About this blog

The Frenchies Files is a personal blog about living with French bulldogs. It's written by Sophie Martin, who has shared her flat with two frenchies — Olive (2014–2023) and Pépin (2023–present) — for the past decade.

## Who writes here

Sophie is a writer based in Lyon, France. By day she works as a technical editor. By morning, evening, and most weekends she walks Pépin, takes him to the dog park, and pretends she doesn't know who's snoring in the room next door at 3 a.m.

## What you'll find

Practical posts about caring for a French bulldog, drawn from personal experience and (where applicable) backed by published veterinary research. Topics include feeding, exercise, training, health, and the small absurdities of life with a brachycephalic companion.

This is not a veterinary publication. Nothing on this site is medical advice. For health questions, see a qualified veterinarian — ideally one familiar with brachycephalic breeds.

## Editorial policy

- **No sponsored content.** When I mention products, they're things I bought with my own money. If that ever changes, the post will say so clearly.
- **No affiliate links.** I don't make money when you click links here.
- **Comments are closed.** Spam is exhausting. The contact form is open.
- **Corrections welcome.** If you spot something wrong, the contact page tells you how to reach me.

## Privacy

The Frenchies Files doesn't track visitors. No analytics scripts, no fingerprinting, no third-party cookies. Just static pages served from a single host.

## Thank you

Thanks for reading. If a post here helps even one frenchie owner skip a vet emergency or pick a better harness, it's been worthwhile.
