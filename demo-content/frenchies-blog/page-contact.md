# Get in touch

If you've spotted an error, want to suggest a topic, or have a question about something I've written, you're welcome to email me.

**Email**: `sophie@frenchies-blog.example`

I try to read every message but I don't always reply quickly — usually within a week or two for non-urgent things. If your dog is unwell, please don't wait for me; call your vet.

## What I can help with

- Spotting and reporting errors in posts
- Suggesting topics you'd like covered
- General French bulldog questions (I'll share what I know — I'm not a vet)
- Permission to quote or translate a post

## What I can't help with

- Medical diagnoses or treatment recommendations
- Breeder recommendations (I don't know any current ones I'd recommend by name; please research carefully and consider adoption from breed-specific rescues)
- Promotional partnerships, sponsored posts, or affiliate arrangements (the blog doesn't accept these)

## Adoption resources

If you're considering adding a French bulldog to your life, please look at adoption first. Many frenchies end up in rescue because their families underestimated the medical costs or the time commitment. A few resources worth checking:

- **The French Bulldog Rescue Network** (rescue-focused in the US)
- **French Bulldog Rescue UK**
- **SOS Bouledogue Français** (France)

Adopt if you can. Buy only from a breeder who health-tests parents and lets you visit the home.
