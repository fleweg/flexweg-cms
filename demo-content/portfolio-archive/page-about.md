# A small studio. A long view.

ARCHIVE is the working name of a three-person studio based out of a former factory in the Marais. We do creative direction, architectural photography, and exhibition design — typically across the same project, typically for the same client across multiple years.

## What we do

![Hands at work](07-hero-detail.jpg)

We're at our best when we get to embed with a client for an extended period — from the initial brief, through the building (or installation, or campaign) phase, to the final monograph or exhibition that closes the loop. A typical project lasts twelve to eighteen months.

We don't do one-off commercial photography, and we don't do social-content production. We charge by the project, not the deliverable, and we take on no more than six projects per year.

## The team

Three of us, full-time. Lila handles creative direction and most client conversations. Marcus is the principal photographer. Mio runs production, archives, and the monograph publishing line.

## Awards & recognition

Selected to FIB Annual 2023. Architectural Photographer of the Year (Marcus), 2022. Monograph Excellence Gold, 2021. ADC Black Cube finalist, 2020.
