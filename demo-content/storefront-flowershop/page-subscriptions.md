# Floral rituals

Most people who sign up for a flower subscription tell us the same thing six months in: it's not the flowers they look forward to, it's the **ritual**. The weekly Wednesday-afternoon untying. The walk past the front door with the wrapped bundle. The decision of where this week's bouquet goes — kitchen window, dining table, bedside.

Flowers are the prop. The ritual is the point.

## The three subscriptions we offer

### Weekly bouquet — the everyday ritual

A bouquet every week, designed by us with whatever's most beautiful at market that morning. Three sizes — £25, £35, or £50 per delivery. Pause for holidays. Switch sizes whenever. We bill quarterly so you don't think about it.

[See it in the shop →](/bouquets/weekly-subscription.html)

### Monthly specials — the punctuation

Once a month, a larger statement bouquet, themed to the season. Peonies in May. Dahlias in August. Branches and amaryllis in December. Two sizes — £60 compact or £100 statement. For people who don't want a weekly habit but want something to mark each month with.

[See it in the shop →](/subscriptions/monthly-specials.html)

### Bi-monthly garden — the slow lane

Every two months, a houseplant or a mixed cuttings pack to start in your own garden. £30 per delivery. For plant people, gardeners-in-training, or anyone allergic to cut flowers but in love with chlorophyll.

[See it in the shop →](/subscriptions/bimonthly-garden.html)

## Try before you commit

We offer a **four-week trial** of the weekly subscription — same bouquet, same delivery day, no commitment beyond the first month. If you stop after week four we'll just stop billing. No phone calls, no retention emails. We respect your time.

To start a trial: [get in touch](/contact.html) with your delivery address and preferred delivery day (Wednesday, Thursday, Friday or Saturday).

## Where we deliver

Free same-day delivery within 10 km of the shop. £8 within 25 km. Outside that, we use a courier and the cost depends on the postcode — the [contact form](/contact.html) is the easiest way to check.

## Pause, gift, switch

The subscription is yours to control:

- **Pausing**: a single email. We pause your billing on the next quarterly cycle.
- **Gifting**: any subscription can be set up to deliver to a different address. Card included with each delivery if you want.
- **Switching sizes**: tell us before the next quarter starts and we adjust.
- **Cancelling**: also a single email. We don't make it harder than it needs to be.

## Why we run subscriptions at all

Honestly: predictable revenue. A small flower shop lives or dies by reliable cash flow, and subscribers smooth out the boom-and-bust of seasons. We're transparent about that. In exchange, subscribers get a 10% break on retail prices and the front-of-queue when limited blooms (peonies, ranunculus, hellebores) come into stock.
