# Contact

## Editorial

For corrections, factual questions, or feedback on a specific piece:

**Email**: `editor@atlas-journal.example`

We try to respond within ten working days. Please name the piece in your subject line.

## Pitches

We accept pitches for long-form travel essays from established writers and emerging voices alike. We are particularly interested in:

- Slow-travel narratives from regions underrepresented in English-language travel writing
- Pieces about specific neighborhoods (rather than entire cities)
- Off-season coverage of well-known destinations
- Train, ferry, and walking journeys
- Critical or contrarian takes on places, where well-supported

We are not interested in:

- Listicles
- "Hidden gems" pieces (the framing is exhausted)
- Hotel reviews
- Anything that mentions Instagram in the first three paragraphs

Send a one-paragraph pitch, three relevant links to previous work, and a sense of how long the finished piece would be. We pay competitive rates for accepted pitches and require exclusive English-language rights for six months from publication.

**Pitches**: `pitches@atlas-journal.example`

## Subscriptions

Atlas Journal is free to read. We accept voluntary monthly support at any amount that feels right to you. Subscribers receive a quarterly print issue (when budget allows) and an early-access newsletter the day before each piece publishes.

Subscribe at: [atlas-journal.example/subscribe](https://atlas-journal.example/subscribe) (placeholder — replace with real link)

## Press

We do not have a press team. For media inquiries, write to `editor@atlas-journal.example` and we'll handle it ourselves.
