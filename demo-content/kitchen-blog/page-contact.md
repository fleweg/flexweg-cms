# Get in touch

If you've spotted an error in a recipe, want to suggest a topic, or have a question about a method, you're welcome to email me.

**Email**: `anna@kitchen-stories.example`

I try to read every message. I don't always reply quickly — usually within a week for non-urgent things. For recipe questions, please describe what you cooked, what you used, what happened, and what oven you have. The more detail, the more useful my answer.

## What I'm happy to help with

- Recipe failure debugging (what to adjust next time)
- Substitution questions (out of buttermilk, no rye flour, etc.)
- Ingredient sourcing in unusual countries
- Spotting errors or typos in posts

## What I can't help with

- Restaurant recommendations beyond the city I live in
- Diet plans, calorie counts, macro tracking — I'm not qualified
- Recipe requests for specific dietary restrictions I haven't cooked for personally
- Promotional partnerships, sponsorships, or affiliate proposals (the blog doesn't accept these)

## Submitting a recipe

If you've adapted something from this blog and it became your own thing, I love hearing about it. Send me the recipe and a couple of photos. I might feature it on a future Reader Recipes post (with full credit to you).
